Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

You can create an account by clicking on the "Sign Up" button on the login page and filling in your details.
Go to the category page and click on "Submit Idea". Fill in the title, description, and category, then click Submit.
Click the thumbs-up icon next to the idea you support. Your vote will be counted immediately.
Navigate to the moderation panel, review the idea, and click "Approve" to publish it.
Use the assignment feature in the idea details view to select a team and assign the idea.
Go to your profile settings and click "Change Password". Enter your current and new password to update.
In your profile settings, navigate to "Notifications" and customize your preferences.
